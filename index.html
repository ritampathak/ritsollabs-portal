International Mathematics Tutoring

Santona Academy

Santona Academy teaches mathematics to students around the world — from school foundations through competitive and olympiad-level problem solving. Classes are small, around five students, so the focus stays on individual attention and mentorship: a real teacher who learns how each student thinks. Alongside teaching, we create our own curriculum and practice tests, using AI to help build them with human review and correction at every step. The academy is named after Santona, our founder's late mother and a lifelong educator, and is built around the patient, personal teaching she stood for.
